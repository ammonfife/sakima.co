This Privacy Policy describes how Sakima ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses your personal information when you use our services, visit our website, interact with us on third-party platforms, or otherwise provide your information to us.

By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

  • Conducting analytics, research, and business intelligence
  • Improving our products, services, and operations

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Any other purpose disclosed at the time of collection

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with third parties including:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., SMS platforms, email providers, payment processors, shipping carriers)

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.
