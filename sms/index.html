SMS Terms & Privacy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

SMS Messaging Program

Sakima offers multiple SMS notification channels. You may subscribe to one or more of the following:

  • Whatnot — Live auction alerts, show reminders, and Whatnot-exclusive deals
  • eBay — New listing alerts, auction updates, and eBay-exclusive offers
  • VIP — Promo codes, early access, and general announcements

By opting in to any channel, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from Sakima on that channel. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Opt-In

You may subscribe by texting any of the following keywords to our SMS number:

  • COIN — Subscribe to all channels (Whatnot + eBay + VIP)
  • WHATNOT — Subscribe to Whatnot alerts only
  • EBAY — Subscribe to eBay alerts only
  • VIP — Subscribe to VIP announcements only
  • YES — Subscribe to all channels

You may also opt in via the signup form on our website at sakima.co.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies by channel. You may receive up to 10 messages per channel per month depending on promotions, events, and new inventory.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your wireless carrier for details about your text messaging plan.

Carrier Liability

Carriers (e.g., T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon) are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to effective transmission from your network operator and is not guaranteed.

Opt-Out

You can manage your subscriptions at any time by texting any of the following:

  • STOP — Unsubscribe from all channels immediately
  • STOP WHATNOT — Unsubscribe from Whatnot alerts only
  • STOP EBAY — Unsubscribe from eBay alerts only
  • STOP VIP — Unsubscribe from VIP announcements only

Replying STOP by itself will always unsubscribe you from all channels. You will receive a confirmation message. To re-subscribe after opting out, text any opt-in keyword above.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at privacy@sakima.co. You may also text STATUS to see which channels you are currently subscribed to.

Privacy

Your phone number and SMS opt-in data will not be shared with any third parties. For our full privacy policy, see sakima.co/privacy.
